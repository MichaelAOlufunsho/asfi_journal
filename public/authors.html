Author Guidelines


Author Guidelines for ASFI Research Journal


Objective:

ASFI Research Journal is dedicated to publishing high-quality, original research that contributes to the advancement of knowledge within the African context. Authors are encouraged to submit manuscripts that adhere to the journal's standards of excellence, promoting scholarly discourse and contributing to the diversity of academic disciplines.


Submission Process:


1. Manuscript Preparation:

Authors should prepare their manuscripts following the journal's specific formatting guidelines, which are available on the journal's website.

Manuscripts should be written in clear, concise English, and must adhere to ethical standards.


2. Online Submission:

Manuscripts should be submitted electronically through the journal's online submission system.

Authors must create an account on the submission platform to track the progress of their submission.


3. Originality and Ethical Considerations:

Authors are responsible for ensuring the originality of their work and should not submit manuscripts that have been previously published or are under consideration elsewhere.

Proper citation and acknowledgment of sources are crucial. Authors should adhere to ethical standards and declare any conflicts of interest.


Peer Review Process:


1. Editorial Evaluation:

Submitted manuscripts undergo an initial evaluation by the Editorial Board to ensure alignment with the journal's scope and guidelines.

Manuscripts meeting initial criteria proceed to the peer review process.


2. Double-Blind Peer Review:

ASFI Research Journal follows a double-blind peer review model, ensuring the anonymity of both authors and reviewers.

Authors should submit a blinded manuscript, removing any identifying information.


3. Feedback and Revisions:

Authors will receive detailed feedback from reviewers and the Editor-in-Chief.

Authors are encouraged to address reviewers' comments and suggestions in a timely manner, submitting a revised manuscript.


Final Decision:

The final decision on acceptance, revision, or rejection is communicated to the authors by the Editor-in-Chief.

manuscripts proceed to the production process, preparing for publication.


Author Responsibilities:


1. Originality and Attribution:

Authors must ensure the originality of their work and appropriately attribute sources.

Any potential conflicts of interest should be disclosed during the submission process.


2. Clarity and Presentation:

Manuscripts should be written with clarity and precision.

Authors should follow the prescribed format and structure, ensuring the logical flow of ideas.


3. Timely Response:

Authors are expected to respond promptly to editorial communications, including revisions and queries.


4. Publication Ethics:

Authors should adhere to ethical standards, including proper citation, data integrity, and compliance with relevant guidelines.
ASFI Research Journal appreciates the contributions of authors in enriching scholarly discourse and advancing knowledge. By adhering to these guidelines, authors play a vital role in maintaining the journal's commitment to excellence and integrity in academic publishing.
