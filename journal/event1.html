ASFI 2023 Virtual Multidisciplinary Conference & Boot Camp

ASFI 2023 Virtual Conference and Boot Camp Research for Africa's Transformation: Challenges, Quality, and Impact


The African Science Frontiers Initiatives (ASFI) held its first-ever virtual conference and boot camp on November 28, 2023. The theme of the conference was "Research for Africa's Transformation: Challenges, Quality, and Impact." The conference brought together leading academicians, scholars, and researchers from across Africa to share their knowledge and exchange ideas on the contribution of research to the development of Africa.


ASFI Boot Camp


The ASFI Boot Camp was an informal occasion for African scientists to come together, discuss, and create opportunities for research capacity building and career development. The Boot Camp featured a series of workshops and panels on topics such as grant writing, research ethics, and scientific communication.


Key highlights of the conference and boot camp


The conference featured keynote presentations from leading African scientists. The boot camp provided practical training and mentorship for African scientists. The conference and boot camp brought together African scientists from across the continent to share their knowledge and ideas.


Conference Proceedings


The proceedings of the conference will be published in an open-access journal. The proceedings will include the full text of the keynote presentations, as well as a selection of papers from the conference.


ASFI's commitment to research


ASFI is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting scientific research in Africa. ASFI provides funding, training, and mentorship to African scientists. ASFI also works to advocate for science and technology in Africa.

The ASFI 2023 Virtual Conference and Boot Camp was a success. The conference and boot camp provided a platform for African scientists to share their knowledge and ideas, and to build relationships with other scientists from across the continent. ASFI is committed to supporting the growth of science and technology in Africa, and the ASFI 2023 Virtual Conference and Boot Camp was an important step in this direction.


About ASFI

The African Science Frontiers Initiative (ASFI) is a pan-African organization that aims to foster a climate of scientific excellence in Africa. ASFI's goal is to create an environment where African scientists can thrive and contribute to the advancement of knowledge and the development of Africa. ASFI supports research in all areas of science and technology, with a focus on areas that are relevant to Africa's development needs.


ASFI's activities include:

  • Providing funding for research projects
  • Offering training and mentorship to African scientists
  • Advocating for science and technology in Africa
  • Promoting collaboration between African scientists and scientists from other parts of the world

ASFI is committed to making Africa a leader in scientific research. ASFI believes that science and technology are essential for Africa's development, and that African scientists have the potential to make a significant contribution to the world.
